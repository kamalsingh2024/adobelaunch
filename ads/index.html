⬇ Install — Do This Once

  1. Show your bookmarks bar:
    Windows: Ctrl + Shift + B  |  Mac: ⌘ + Shift + B
  2. Drag the button below into your bookmarks bar

AD Debugger
💡 Can't drag? Right-click the button → Copy Link → add a bookmark manually and paste it as the URL.

▶ How to Use

  1. Navigate to the 🏈 AFL Network website or the specific page you need to test
  2. Wait for the page to fully load
  3. Click AD Debugger in your bookmarks bar
  4. The debugger panel will appear on screen — interact with ads as normal and watch the data populate in real time
  5. To re-run on a new page, simply navigate there and click the bookmarklet again
🔁 The tool is always up to date. Every click pulls the latest version automatically — no reinstall needed.

🔧 Troubleshooting

  • Panel doesn't appear — Refresh the page and click the bookmarklet again
  • Blank or broken panel — Disable your ad blocker on this page and retry
  • Nothing happens at all — Make sure the page has fully loaded before clicking
